Real Estate Management

All-in-one solution to manage properties, contracts, collections, maintenance and renovations.

Central Property Hub

One single record per property with photos, geolocation, physical characteristics and a real-time financial summary (collected / pending / overdue / net balance).

Contracts & Collections

Rental and sale contracts with an automatic collection schedule. A daily cron flags overdue payments and turns delinquent contracts red across the UI.

Maintenance Tickets

Report incidents per property, assign priorities and providers, and track resolution times. Costs feed back into the property financial summary.

Renovation Projects

Plan refurbishments and furniture purchases as projects with tasks and a budget. Active renovations show on the dashboard.

Dashboard at a Glance

Tile-based overview: properties, available units, contracts, active rate, tenants, owners, providers, rental & sales analytics per status, overdue collections, open tickets and active renovations. Every tile is clickable to drill down.
